Open Positions

Licensed Electrician

Full-time Orange County, CA

We're seeking a skilled Licensed Electrician with experience in residential and commercial electrical work. The ideal candidate will have strong technical skills, attention to detail, and commitment to safety.

Requirements:

  • Valid California Electrical License (C-10)
  • 3+ years of electrical experience
  • Strong knowledge of electrical codes and regulations
  • Excellent problem-solving abilities
  • Valid driver's license and clean driving record
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Responsibilities:

  • Install, maintain, and repair electrical systems
  • Read blueprints and technical diagrams
  • Ensure compliance with safety standards
  • Communicate effectively with clients
  • Complete projects on time and within budget

Electrical Apprentice

Full-time Orange County, CA

Join our apprenticeship program to learn from experienced electricians while working on real projects. Perfect opportunity for those starting their electrical career.

Requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Enrolled or willing to enroll in electrical apprenticeship program
  • Strong work ethic and willingness to learn
  • Physical ability to perform electrical work
  • Basic math and reading skills
  • Valid driver's license preferred

Responsibilities:

  • Assist licensed electricians with installations and repairs
  • Learn electrical codes and safety procedures
  • Maintain tools and equipment
  • Follow instructions and safety guidelines
  • Participate in training programs

Service Electrician

Full-time Orange County, CA

We need an experienced Service Electrician to handle troubleshooting, repairs, and maintenance calls. Customer service skills are essential for this client-facing role.

Requirements:

  • Valid California Electrical License (C-10)
  • 5+ years of service and repair experience
  • Excellent diagnostic and troubleshooting skills
  • Strong customer service abilities
  • Professional appearance and communication
  • Own reliable vehicle and tools

Responsibilities:

  • Respond to service calls and emergencies
  • Diagnose electrical problems accurately
  • Provide clear explanations to customers
  • Complete repairs efficiently and safely
  • Maintain service vehicle and inventory

Project Manager / Estimator

Full-time Orange County, CA

Lead electrical projects from estimation to completion. This role requires strong organizational skills, electrical knowledge, and project management experience.

Requirements:

  • Electrical background or related experience
  • 3+ years of project management experience
  • Experience with estimating software
  • Strong communication and negotiation skills
  • Ability to read blueprints and technical drawings
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office and project tools

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare accurate project estimates
  • Manage project timelines and budgets
  • Coordinate with clients, vendors, and team members
  • Track project progress and reporting
  • Ensure quality and safety standards
